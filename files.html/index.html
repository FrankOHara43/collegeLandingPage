Digha-Ashiana Road, Digha Ghat, Patna-800011

Our mission is to engage in extensive action-based research and scholarly studies that thoroughly explore the diverse and complex aspects of life in Bihar. This involves examining the social, economic, political, cultural, educational, and religious realities experienced by its people. We place a special emphasis on understanding and addressing the unique needs and challenges faced by the most marginalized and underrepresented communities in the region, aiming to create meaningful and lasting impact through our research and initiatives.

Four Fold Strategy of XISR

The 4-fold strategy of our institute focuses on Awareness Creation, Empowerment, Alliance and Networking and Social Advocacy

Awareness Creation

Create awareness among the rural masses, marginalized communities, etc

Empowerment

Understand the fundamental issues that affect the marginalized communities and in collaboration with other towards their empowerment.

Alliance and Networking

Being a resource centre, XISR collaborates with other social centres that work for the education and empowerment of the marginalized communities.

Social Advocacy

Through field based studies, documentation and data collection, XISR attempts to advocate the cause of the most marginalized segment of the population in partnership with others.c

About Us

Xavier Institute of Social Research (XISR), a centre for social science, training, networking, lobbying and action,
is located in St. Xavier’s College of Management and Technology campus, Digha-Ashiyana Road, Patna. It is established by the Jesuits of Patna Province.
It aims at creating an interface between researchers, scholars, academics and activists and initiating a process of social awakening through critical reflection and alternative discourse
thereby contributing to strengthening people’s struggles towards creating a just and humane social order.
